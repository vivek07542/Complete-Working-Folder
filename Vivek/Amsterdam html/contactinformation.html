Organization

How does the Municipality of Amsterdam work?

The municipal organization consists of 4 clusters, an administrative and corporate staff and 7 districts.

The clusters have expertise in a specific field, such as sports, youth or parking. They elaborate policy into city-wide frameworks within which the districts can do the executive work. They also offer direct support to residents who need it, for example in the area of ​​participation or work.

Board and Organization

Board and Organization is a strategic, high-quality, flexible, professional and result-oriented organization, which primarily provides advice and support to the college, the mayor, the council, the municipal secretary and the municipal management team.


Lake

Cluster Services and Information

Services and Information have the demands of the city of today and tomorrow at the forefront. The cluster provides services to citizens, residents, entrepreneurs and visitors to the municipality of Amsterdam, provides overview, knowledge and comprehensive information.


Lake

Cluster Internal Services

Internal Services offers flexible and reliable facilities to colleagues and the board. For example, employees of Internal Services provide up-to-date management information, they ensure the introduction of equal workplaces throughout the city, they facilitate various activities and they advise the board and the organization on decisions to be made.


Lake

Cluster Space and Economy

Space and Economy stands for the creation of the spatial and economic preconditions necessary for Amsterdam to grow into a strong metropolis. The cluster does this based on challenges in the spatial economic sector.

Lake

Cluster Social

The Municipality of Amsterdam has great responsibilities in areas such as care, youth and work. Such as education, health, diversity, anti-discrimination, youth unemployment, sports, the Sporthallen Zuid, the Amsterdamse Bos, poverty alleviation, debt counseling and much more.

Lake

City management cluster

Stadsbeheer is responsible for the management, supervision and enforcement of the quality of life in the Amsterdam public space. Every day, employees of Stadsbeheer ensure that the city is clean, whole, safe and sustainable. We work in a smart way with each other and with our chain partners. So that we are where and when the city needs us most.

Lake

City District

The municipality of Amsterdam has seven districts: Center, New West, North, East, West, South, Southeast. The urban district organizations are mainly focused on the implementation of urban policy.

Lake

Other entities and participations

The Municipality of Amsterdam has several organizational units that do not belong to the clusters.
