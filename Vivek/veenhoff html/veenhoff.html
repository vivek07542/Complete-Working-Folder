Welcome!

Veenhof Notariaat is a small Amsterdam notary office that stands for a personal and involved approach.

The office consists of four employees, which means there are short lines and a direct service.

From 1 January 2019, the name has been changed from Faasen Notariaat to Veenhof Notariaat.

Feel free to contact us to get acquainted or to request a quote.

The Veenhof Notariaat team is happy to assist you!
